Charlie Golonkiewicz

Charlie Golonkiewicz is a Brooklyn-based glass artist, whose work is inspired by traditional Venetian glassmaking techniques. He currently works as a freelance glassblower for several artists and designers in New York, as well as teaching glassblowing lessons to the public.
